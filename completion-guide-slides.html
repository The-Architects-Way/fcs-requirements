Before You Begin

Getting Started
What You Will Need
Before starting the form, please have the following ready:
  • School logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG format -- high resolution preferred)
  • School crest / emblem if different from the logo
  • Official school colours (colour names or hex codes if known)
  • Campus photos -- buildings, classrooms, facilities, events, students
  • School documents -- prospectus, brochure, admission forms
  • Social media URLs -- Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc.
  • Domain login details -- if you already own a domain name
  • Primary contact person -- name and role at the school
How to Access the Form
Form URL: is.gd/Miraloyd_FCS
